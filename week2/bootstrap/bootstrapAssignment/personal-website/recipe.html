Samosa recipe :

How to make the Samosa Recipe:
Potato filling for Samosa:
  • 4 medium sized potatoes
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 8 cashews (optional)
  • 2 tsp raisins (optional)
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp minced ginger or garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies chopped
  • Pinch of asafoetida
  • 1/2 tsp amchur powder or mango powder (or lemon juice)
  • 1 tsp anardana (dry pomegranate seeds)
  • Few coriander leaves chopped


Next Step
How to make Samosa:
  1. Lightly knead the dough to smoothen a bit. Make 4 to 5 portions of the dough and rool to balls.
  2. Grease the rolling area and then flatten a ball. Begin to roll to a oval shape. Cut it to two. If the edges are too thick, gently roll it to thin down. Smear water over the straight edge. Join the edges to make a cone. Press gently to seal the cone.
  3. Fill the cone with potato masala and smear water on the edges. Bring the edges together and seal it. Make sure the Samosa has been sealed well.
  4. Finish making all the Samosas.
